WARNING

If you watch enough skate tutorials on YouTube, you will eventually start seeing ads for skater trainers, shown above.Those may seem helpful, but those are what NOT to buy. You may think these will help with your fear of falling and help you get more comfortable learning tricks, but getting comfortable riding on your board is almost crucial in seeing progression. This mechanism stops your wheels from moving, and in the eyes of many including me, a skateboard that can’t move is not a skateboard at all. The feel of a board stationary vs. rolling is very different, so the muscle memory you gain from learning tricks stationary will most likely be lost when you take the trainers off. Another thing to note: The more comfortable you are riding a board, the more control you have over it, and the less chance you have of falling.
