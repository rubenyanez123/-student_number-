Why I Started Skateboarding

In the years before I started, (2016-2018) I was pretty intrigued by skateboarding. One of the reasons I started was because of my brother, and another honest reason was because I thought I would fit in with the cool kids more. So I bought a board, started learning in my driveway, and the rest is history. I am very glad I started, because it is now my favorite method of physical exercise, and it’s how I made most of my current friends. Looking back, my only regret is not starting earlier. I don’t know why I was afraid to ask my parents for a skateboard for so long.
