About Us




Skate Haarlem is a website that was created in the fall of 2023 by Ruben Yanez, a Dutch-American student at Inholland Hogeschool Haarlem. He hopes to get more people interested in his passion.

Our mission is to bring skateboarding and its footprint in Haarlem to the attention of international students in the area. in many ways, skateboarding has a lot of potential to be bigger than it is. It is a fun and healthy activity that I think many would like to learn how to do, but unfortunately many shy away from it because of the intimidation that comes along with A) the sometimes-judgemental community, and B) the risk factor of it, whether it be because of stories these students have heard of people injuring themselves or videos they see on the internet. My goal is to lessen these fears and inspire people to get on the board, as well as get to know Haarlem better and even see it in a new light.
